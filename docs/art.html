Digital Art and Character Design

Personal Works

Below is a gallery of some of my personal digital works. I created these using Procreate, a digital drawing app. I used different brushes, layers, and colors to create these. A pass time I really enjoy is character design, the work I display here are all original characters.

A superhero called Blazeheart lights a beacon of hope every morning.
Tey'Lya lives in a space port city called Small Apple. She tried modding herself with new tech but it proved to be faulty and it requires being charged regularly.
Harmony and her friend Gizmo live in a magical city. They regularly cause trouble for their own amusement.
The Tomb Twins are rulers of a near forgotten settlement.
The Junkrunner lives under the rule of the Tomb Twins. He harnesses "Raw Speed" to be able to run at super speeds.
Oce, the sea titan, can often be caught watching human activity.
Aquilo, Mother Silvana, Oce are the three titans of nature. Each protect the skys, the earth, and the seas respectively.
A comic style portrait of a superhero called Kurrent. Background photo was taken by me.
This supervillian, called Solar Flare, only seeks revenge. She can control light.
A superhero sidekick called Satellite.
Lemon is a weapons engineer, she often works with superheros to create any gear they may need.
Nova is Lemons younger sister. She stole some of the tech Lemon created and ran off to create a gang called the Neo-Amazons.
A concept design of a mysterious character that is always accompanied with clouds of mist.
