Photography and Video Art

Video Art

I took this course in my junior year, the fall of 2021. This course taught me about using film equipment and the production needed to make video art. Throughout the course I learned how to use the Blackmagic 4k film camera, the RED Dragon 6k film camera, lights, greenscreens, and the post-production.

Project Statement: The video starts off with several scenes and nothing seems to be wrong. But the character has a suspicion that something is off. They die in the nightmare and wake up shaken but they don't quite remember what happened, the nightmare repeats into real life.

Song: Heartbeat of the hood from YouTube Audio Library

I used a 360 camera in a cardboard and paper scene to create a 3-dimensional space. I was then able to animate onto this 3D space. The viewer can orbit around the scene.

Photography

I took this course in the Fall 2020 Semester, of my Sophomore year. In this course I learned and practiced the concepts of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, and photography. With the two house examples I practiced using the clone stamp tool to correct blemishes in the original photo. In the second set of photos, the Runner, I practiced selecting parts of a photo, creating new layers with the selection and then editing the selection. I can select the shirt and the headband separately and move them onto different layers. Then I can go into the color settings and change the grey into the desired color. With the VNet building I practiced the clone stamp tool, brush tool, as well as different ways to use the text tool. I used the text tool to type the words and then I created a shadow behind the text. Using the brush tool, I could create the illusion that I made 3D text.

The last 3 photos are some that I took myself and edited the contrast and saturation using Adobe Lightroom.
